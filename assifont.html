Designing for Readability

Why Typography Matters in Web Design

Typography is one of the most important aspects of user experience on the web. The right font pairing, spacing, and layout can drastically improve how users perceive and interact with your content.

Using distinct fonts for headings and body text creates a visual hierarchy, helping readers scan and understand content faster. For instance, a serif font like Merriweather for titles adds elegance and contrast, while a sans-serif font like Open Sans ensures body text is readable on all screen sizes.

Additionally, proper line-height and spacing reduce eye strain and improve focus. Always test your typography choices on different screen sizes to maintain a smooth and enjoyable reading experience.
